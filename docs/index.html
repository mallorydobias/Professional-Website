About My Work

Using a wide range of research methods and designs, my work makes one thing clear. Accessing mental health treatment shouldn't be so hard. Many youth in need of support do not access mental health care. Many others drop out after receiving a handful of sessions, or have negative experiences in treatment. For more than a decade, I've studied accessible, affirming, non-invasive, and evidence-based supports for young people.

Randomized Trials

Develop and evaluate accessible, youth-centered mental health interventions using gold standard methods.

Content Analysis

Examine youth feedback about their own mental health support to inform product decisions.

User Engagement Analysis

Assess patterns in youth engagement and churn to inform future intervention iteration.

Predictive Modeling

Predict which youth are less likely to access care using advanced computational methods.
